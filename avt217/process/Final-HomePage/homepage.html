Where The Horizon Disappears.

Uyuni is one of the most popular tourist spots in Bolivia, with it being home to the Salar de Uyuni, which is the largest salt flat in the world.

Uyuni is a part of Potosi department in the south-west part of Bolivia and is known for being mountainous and rather desolate as it is primarily a trading post between the major Bolivian cities and Argentina.
